CRISTIAN Martín Echenique

A 35-year-old Argentinian, emigrated to the United Arab Emirates 2 years ago knowing that the local soccer market is very virgin and there is a very big projection for the future in soccer.
I founded GIE FC in 2021 together with my partner local, betting on the growth and development of children's and professional football in the next 5 years, opening the international market of South American players, especially Argentines, and opening the market of players from the African continent with talented players, the vision we seek is to project the Argentine game in the United Arab Emirates, we are the first Argentine team with Argentine roots and tactics in the UAE with Argentine players, Local Africans projecting for the next season to be champion and go up to 1 division and to be able to play in the future the proleague with the evil high team soccer of the league, we have two teams in 2 division GIE FC and Solano City.

Mohamed Sebil Ghazi Khamis Almadani

I am born in the United Arab Emirates, currently 31 years old, visionary and entrepreneur, professional football player: former Shabab Club player (current Shabab Al Ahli) Champion with Quatro FC 2 division 2019, moving to Al Ittifaq fc, currently player and founder of GIE FC, the vision we have is that in the coming years we will be one of the 10 best teams in the UAE and open the market to players from Latin America.

Captain Khalil Ibrahim Mohamed Alsaqay

Professional Manager Team, Born in the United Arab Emirates former Shabab Club player (current Shabab Al Ahli) former UAEFA official in 2 division, currently working at GIE FC as Manager team,
My mission is to leave GIE FC in a good position in the sports and academic framework in the United Arab Emirates, show solidarity with players such as Argentines, Brazilians, Paraguay, Uruguay and all of Africa and open markets to excellent players and people.

Ashwin Nelappana

I am born in the United Arab Emirates of Indian family descendants, coach the U21 national B license, former coach U13 Al Nasr club, trusting in the opportunity we have at GIE FC to continue growing and be one of the best Coach in the league and being able to lead the national team, I thank GIE FC for the great opportunity they gave me to take on the position in the U21 squad.
We aspire to be champions and the best.
